About Us

Welcome to Anna University

Anna University is a renowned technical university located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1978, it was initially named the "Perarignar Anna University of Technology" after C.N. Annadurai, the former Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu. However, in 1982, it was renamed Anna University in his honor.

Anna University is recognized as one of the premier technical universities in India and offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields of engineering, technology, and allied sciences. The university has numerous affiliated colleges across the state of Tamil Nadu, including government and private institutions.
